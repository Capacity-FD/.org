VISION

Empowered Communities for a Sustainable Future.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide accessible and high-quality skill-building programs tailored to meet specific industry needs and local job markets.

PROGRAMS

1. Education and Early Childhood Development.

We enhance access to quality education and early childhood development for a strong learning foundation. This includes establishing early learning centers in underserved communities and equipping these learning centers with qualified educators and age-appropriate resources.

2. Innovation

This program involves fostering a culture of innovation that drives economic and social progress within communities.

3. Sports and Arts

Through this program we catalyze personal growth, community cohesion, and cultural expression through the support and promotion of sports and arts.

4. Consultancy

We provide expert consultancy services to individuals, communities, and organizations seeking guidance in their development endeavors.

5. Environment and Agriculture

We advocate for sustainable environmental practices and promoting agriculture as a means of livelihood and food security.

About Us

Welcome to Capacity For Development website!

We are a non-profit organization based in the Township of Zolozolo in Mzuzu City, Malawi. Our mission is to provide support and resources to help the people of our community thrive.

Since our founding in July, 2023, we have been working hard to make a difference. We have a dedicated team of staff and volunteers who are passionate about helping others. We offer a variety of programs and services, including educational opportunities, Agriculture and environment, and more. We are also committed to transparency and accountability, and we are always looking for ways to improve our work.

We are grateful for the support we have received from individuals, businesses, and organizations around the world. Without this support, we would not be able to do the work that we do. We invite you to learn more about our organization and consider supporting our work. Thank you for visiting our website!
